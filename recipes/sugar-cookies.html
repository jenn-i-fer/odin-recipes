Sugar Cookies Recipe

Description

describe recipe

Ingredients

  • 340g Butter
  • 300g Sugar, white
  • 562g Flour, all-purpose
  • 4g Baking Powder
  • 3.5g Salt
  • 30g Vanilla Extract
  • 2g Almond Extract, optional
  • 2 Eggs

Steps

  1. Using the paddle attachment on a stand mixer, cream together butter and sugar on mid-high until light and fluffy.
  2. In a separate large bowl, whisk together dry ingredients (flour, baking powder, salt). Set aside.
  3. To creamed butter and sugar, add eggs, vanilla extract, and almond extract. Mix on medium until well-combined and fluffy.
  4. Gradually add dry ingredients on low until just combined.(NOTE: Dough will be very soft. If it's too soft or sticky, add an extra 5 to 10 grams of flour).
  5. Divide dough into 2 portions. Flatten each into a 1" disk and wrap tightly in plastic wrap. Chill for 1 hour (up to 3 days) in the fridge.
  6. Roll out your dough to about 1/8" to 1/4" and cut it into your desired shapes (rough onto a lightly floured surface or between sheets of parchement paper/plastic wrap).
  7. Bake 11 to 12 minutues (until cookies are lightly browned around the edges). Remove from oven and let cool for 5 minutes on sheet pan then transfer to a wire rack.
